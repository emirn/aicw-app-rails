## Strategic Guidelines

### 1. Topic Organization
- Group related articles together using folder paths (e.g., `/category/article-name`)
- Build topical authority by covering related topics comprehensively
- Avoid keyword cannibalization across articles

### 2. Content Diversity
- Mix educational content (how-to guides, tutorials) with commercial content (comparisons, reviews)
- Balance broad awareness topics with specific solution-focused articles
- Include variety: guides, comparisons, lists, tutorials

### 3. Internal Linking Strategy
- Identify hub articles (pillar content) and related spoke articles
- Recommend anchor text for each internal link
- Build connections between related articles

### 4. Keyword Research
- Assign 3-5 target keywords per article
- Include primary keyword + semantically related keywords
- Consider long-tail variations
- Ensure no keyword cannibalization across articles

### 5. Word Count
- Standard articles: 1500-2500 words
- In-depth guides: 2500-4000 words
- Quick reference: 1000-1500 words
